DoNotNotify

Silence the noise. Keep what matters.

What it does

DoNotNotify helps you block annoying or unwanted Android notifications using simple filters — all processed locally on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no data collection.

Get it on Google Play

Key Features

  • Block notifications by app or keyword (supports regex).
  • All processing happens on your device — nothing leaves it.
  • No account, no ads, no analytics.
